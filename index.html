Donald Trump V Logic

The Premise -

Our current president has made history with his comments twoards just about anything.
Some people even take his words at face value! Our goal for this project was to expose
the fallacies in his logic and reasoning.



The Mechanics

Our game works on a very simple mechanic - The recognition of a fallacy - If the player gets one wrong
then their scandal bar will go down, get one right and the bar will go up. As the game starts you are faced
with opponents that are along the lines of trumps political affiliation, but as the game progresses you get to
fight the progressives! Dont let logic that makes actual sense get to you! You're Donald J. Trump after all!
